Server Rules

Follow these rules to keep KadduMC fun for everyone.

01
No Hacking / CheatingXray, kill-aura, fly hacks, or any advantage-giving client modifications are strictly banned.

03
No GriefingDestroying or stealing from other players' builds is not allowed without explicit permission.
04
No SpammingDon't flood chat, use caps excessively, or advertise other servers.

Listen to StaffDecisions made by moderators and admins are final. Open a ticket on Discord for disputes.

Have Fun!This is a community built on passion. Enjoy yourself and help others do the same.
